Esmé Dance Company

Esmé Dance Company was founded by Veronica Potter in August 2014.
Esmé is a group of artists who are dedicated to the art of dance and sharing their passion.

From the Dancers

"Esmé means to be loved, to be esteemed. Our version of Esmé is to have love for one another, to encourage one another. We focus on the artistry, the love for dance- why we began in the first place. Our company will grow together and share our passion with others."
-  Esmé Dance Company
